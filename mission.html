Our Mission

Renegade of Funk Creations is an independent, artist-led initiative dedicated to using digital art, symbolic storytelling, and creative expression as tools for emotional healing, reflection, and community support. Influenced by surrealist and visionary traditions, this project creates spaces where people can explore their inner world safely and at their own pace.

This effort is currently in its early stages of becoming an official nonprofit organization. The long-term goal is to provide accessible art-based support for veterans, disabled individuals, people in recovery, and anyone seeking a creative outlet for emotional expression. This project is not a business venture and will never be monetized in ways that compromise its integrity or trauma-informed mission.
